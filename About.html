About

Haugheys Clothing's vision is to create affordable, styley and fashionable streetwear that enables customers to represent where they come from or places they love in New Zealand. Haugheys Clothing was inspired by the street I was raised with in Central Auckland. The dream of creating a future where we no longer have to struggle was vital. and giving those an opportunity to wear clothing to represent where they come from that's styley, comfortable and fashionable will not only help our people look superfly but also inhances their confidence to connect with their roots and stomping gronds.

Haughey Clothing creates clothing for people from all walks of life and all ages. Haugheys Clothing
